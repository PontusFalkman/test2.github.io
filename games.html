2D Stealth Platformer

Overview

Outsmart, don't overpower. This is a game of wits, not weapons. Master the environment by manipulating light and sound to slip past vigilant guards. Your goal is to be a ghost—a whisper in the shadows—as you navigate each challenge without leaving a trace.

Play Download Repo

Highlights

  • Master Your Environment: Use dynamic light and darkness to your advantage, sticking to the shadows to remain unseen.
  • Manipulate and Distract: Interact with the world to create diversions. Open doors to create new paths or throw objects to draw attention away.
  • Outmaneuver Attentive Guards: The AI is designed to be observant and challenging. You'll need to study their patterns and exploit their weaknesses to succeed.
  • Agile Traversal: A new climbing system gives you the freedom to explore vertically and find creative routes to outflank your opponents.

Tech Notes

  • Dynamic Visibility System: A centralized `VisibilityField` calculates sightlines, allowing for complex and realistic AI perception.
  • Granular Noise System: Every action, from a footstep to a creaky ladder, generates noise that can alert nearby guards.
  • Advanced AI Perception: The `PerceptionModel` gives guards a probabilistic understanding of their surroundings, making them feel more alive.
  • Interactive Framework: A robust `Interactor` system allows for deep and meaningful manipulation of the game world.
