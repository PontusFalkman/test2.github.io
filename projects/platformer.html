2D Platformer

Personal hobby project. Built off-hours on personal equipment.

Overview

Three levels: classic, dark, and ice with slide mechanics. Double jump, hazards, and door-to-finish.

Play Download (ZIP) Repo

Highlights

  • Double jump, sliding on ice, instant-death hazards.
  • Camera follow, checkpoints, level transitions.
  • Simple HUD and audio settings.

Tech Notes

  • Unity 6 LTS, C#, Input System.
  • GameManager for lives, score, progression.
  • Pixel-perfect camera and tuned physics.

Learning and practice artifact. No commercial use.
